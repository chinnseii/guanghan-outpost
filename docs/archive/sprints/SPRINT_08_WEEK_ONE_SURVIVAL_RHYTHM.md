# Sprint 08: Week One Survival Rhythm

《广寒前哨 Guanghan Outpost》

Version: 0.1  
Status: First playable pass

## Goal

Sprint 08 extends the opening vertical slice from Day 03 to Day 07 and establishes the first reusable long-term rhythm:

- wake up
- read morning status
- check the central console
- perform the day focus
- confirm the last plant is still alive
- send an Earth report
- rest

The goal is not to add a complete survival simulation. The goal is to show that staying alive on the Moon is repeated confirmation over time.

## Daily Focus

- Day 03: stabilize routine and confirm repeated inspection.
- Day 04: water-cycle concern.
- Day 05: power-load limit.
- Day 06: slight last-plant recovery signal.
- Day 07: first week review and weekly Earth report.
